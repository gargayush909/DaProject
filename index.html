About Us

Urgig is a decentralized platform for freelancers and gig posters. Our goal is to provide a transparent and secure platform that allows freelancers and gig posters to connect directly with each other without any third-party interference.

With Urgig, all transactions are recorded on a blockchain, ensuring that all interactions between gig posters and freelancers are secure and transparent. Our platform offers a range of tools and services to help freelancers and gig posters find the right match for their needs.
